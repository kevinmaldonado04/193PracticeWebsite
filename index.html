Cybersecurity Awareness

In today’s digital world, understanding cybersecurity is more important than ever. Learning how to protect your data and devices helps keep you and your community safe. Visit CISA’s website to explore guides on online safety and best practices.

Website Sitemap

This sitemap outlines the planned structure and features for the full Cybersecurity Awareness website:

  • Home – Overview of cybersecurity and its importance.
  • Learn – Tutorials and articles on online safety, phishing prevention, and password hygiene.
  • News – Latest updates on cyber threats and security innovations.
  • Resources – Links to trusted organizations and downloadable safety checklists.
  • Contact/Report – Form for users to report suspicious online activities or ask security questions.
